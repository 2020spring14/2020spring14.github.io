what is web accessibility?

  • having web pages that are accessible to everyone is extremely important. the sad truth is, many webpages are not equipped for those with impaired vision. an estimated 285 million people in the world are visually impaired, including poor eyesight, blindness, and color blindness. thankfully, webpages are becoming more accessible for the impaired. there are now websites that will quickly convert a webpage for the imapired and websites created with everybody in mind.

what do these resources do?

  • these websites are to aid the visually impaired. the websites linked go in more depth about how critical web accessibility is, but they also are there to help the visually impaired. these pages filter the colors for the colorblind and generate the average website to an accessible one.
