The Fame Monster

After the huge success of her debut album "The Fame," released in 2008, Gaga would go onto releassing yet another massive project the following year. The Fame Monster is what Gaga describes as the more true and gritty version of "The Fame". "The Fame" explored satrical interpretations of fame while "The Fame Monster" explores the actual expirences and emotions of fame, since Gaga had just blasted into superstardom. "The Fame Monster" not only brought Gaga one of her most popular songs, it started a cultural revolution with the hit "Bad Romance". This album cemented Gaga as a force to be reckoned with and an icon forever. She went on to tour massive areans and started work on the world-changing "Born This Way". Born This Way
